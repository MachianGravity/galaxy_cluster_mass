Tully Fisher Plot

MachianGravity/galaxy_cluster_mass/tree/main/Reiprich

We present here the radial mass profiles for 106 galaxy clusters, taken from Reiprich_2003 paper. The greenish-yellow curve shows the baryonic mass of each cluster. The solid red curve depicts the Newtonian dynamical mass. The dotted yellow-ochre curve represents the dynamical mass predicted by the Machian Gravity framework. The comparison demonstrates that these different mass estimators are broadly consistent with one another. We also plot, as a black dotted line, a fitted profile obtained by fixing $M_c = 2.759\times 10^{15} M_{\odot}$ and $\lambda^{-1} = 0.4949 r_c$.
